# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how **LinkedIn Translator** collects, uses, and protects your information.

LinkedIn Translator is an independent product and is not affiliated with or endorsed by LinkedIn Corporation.

## 2. Information We Collect

- Account data: email and profile details used for login, account management, and support
- User content: text inputs you submit and generated outputs returned by the Service
- Usage data: device, browser, IP, logs, and analytics events
- Billing data: payment processing is handled by Creem; we do not store full card details

## 3. How We Use Information

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
- Operate billing, credits, and account features
- Detect abuse, fraud, and policy violations
- Respond to support and legal requests
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers (for example hosting, analytics, and payments) only as needed to operate the Service and comply with law.

## 5. Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as needed for service delivery, security, legal compliance, and legitimate business operations.

## 6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data.
